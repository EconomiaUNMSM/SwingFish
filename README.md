## 💡 Quick Overview

### Why SwingFish?
Unlike generalist LLMs that rely on web scraping and news summarization, **SwingFish combats AI hallucinations** by separating raw data extraction from logical reasoning. Our agents interpret pre-verified institutional data (financial statements, Greeks, macro flows) within a strict committee framework.

### The Three Pillars
1.  **Data Provider Engine (Dashboard)**: Pure institutional extraction.
2.  **Abyssal Probe (Multiagent)**: 6 expert agents orchestrated via LangGraph.
3.  **S&P 500 Scoring**: Mathematical filter based on solvency and momentum.
